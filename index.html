Quick Glance

Luncher was created because there are kids today in this country who go without student lunches. Luncher allows schools to broadcast the needs of their students by declaring an amount of donations that they would need to be fullfilled in order to provide lunches for those that go without.

Features

Check out some of our current features. We are always building and updating features to make your experience with Luncher the best.

School Admin

Schools can create a profile via Luncher and request funds as needed so none of their students go without meals.

Donors

You can create an account today and donate to any school listed currently listed within the Luncher app.

Security

With electronic transactions occurring constantly your privacy and security is top of mind to our developers.

The current and future generation of students shouldn't have to a full day of school without a meal. Today you can help a child get through a long day of school on a recharged mind and a satisfied belly. Create your account and start helping !
